<p><p>Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) pumps are mechanical devices designed to prevent blood clots in the deep veins, particularly in patients who are immobile or at risk of developing DVT. These pumps work by applying intermittent pneumatic compression to the legs, facilitating venous blood flow and reducing the likelihood of clot formation. The market for DVT pumps is experiencing significant growth due to increasing awareness of the condition, a rising aging population, and a growing number of surgical procedures that necessitate prophylactic measures against DVT.</p><p>Technological advancements, such as the development of portable and user-friendly devices, are also driving market expansion.
