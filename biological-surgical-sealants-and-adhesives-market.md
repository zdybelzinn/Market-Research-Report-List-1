<p><p>The biological surgical sealants and adhesives market is categorized into fibrin-based, collagen-based, and albumin-based products. Fibrin-based sealants mimic the natural coagulation process, promoting wound healing and tissue repair. Collagen-based adhesives utilize collagen's biocompatibility to bond tissues effectively, enhancing regeneration. Albumin-based sealants leverage the protein’s adhesive properties, offering flexibility and stability in various surgical applications. Each type serves unique roles in enhancing surgical outcomes by facilitating tissue adhesion and reducing complications during procedures.</p></p>
