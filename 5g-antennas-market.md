<p><p>The 5G antennas market includes various types, notably switched multi-beam antennas and adaptive array antennas. Switched multi-beam antennas utilize multiple beams to direct signals, enhancing coverage and efficiency across user locations. In contrast, adaptive array antennas dynamically adjust their radiation patterns based on real-time signal conditions, optimizing performance and capacity. Both types play vital roles in improving 5G network reliability and speed, catering to the growing demand for high-capacity wireless communication in diverse environments.</p></p>
