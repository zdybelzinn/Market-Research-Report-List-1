<p><ul><li>Interior Planning</li><li>Outdoor Planning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>5G network planning and design encompass two main market types: interior planning and outdoor planning. Interior planning focuses on optimizing network performance within buildings, tackling challenges like signal attenuation and ensuring coverage in crowded spaces. It involves deploying small cells and appropriate technologies to enhance indoor connectivity. Outdoor planning, on the other hand, addresses broader coverage areas, optimizing base station placements, and addressing environmental factors like urban density and topography. Both are essential for achieving seamless 5G service delivery.</p></p>
