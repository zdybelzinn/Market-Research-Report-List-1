<p><strong>The Self-Injection Delivery Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intramuscular</li><li>Intravenous</li><li>Sub-Cutaneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-injection delivery systems encompass three main market types: intramuscular, intravenous, and subcutaneous. Intramuscular systems allow for deep tissue injections, ensuring rapid absorption for certain medications. Intravenous systems deliver drugs directly into the bloodstream, facilitating immediate effects, often used in emergency and hospital settings. Subcutaneous systems administer drugs into the fatty tissue just beneath the skin, making them suitable for long-term treatments like insulin. Each type offers distinct benefits tailored to different therapeutic needs and patient preferences.</p></p>
