<p><p>The 1:2 Metal Complex Dyes market is primarily categorized into two types: Non-sulfonated Type and Mono-sulfonated Type. Non-sulfonated Type dyes are characterized by their lack of sulfonic acid groups, offering good lightfastness and washfastness, making them suitable for high-performance textiles. In contrast, Mono-sulfonated Type dyes contain one sulfonic acid group, enhancing water solubility and application versatility while still maintaining decent fastness properties. Both types are utilized in various industries, including textiles, inks, and coatings.</p></p>
