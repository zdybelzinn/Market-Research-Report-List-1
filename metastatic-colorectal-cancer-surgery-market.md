<p><strong>The Metastatic Colorectal Cancer Surgery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tumor Resection</li><li>Portal Vein Embolization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metastatic colorectal cancer surgery market includes two primary types: tumor resection and portal vein embolization. Tumor resection involves surgically removing the cancerous part of the colon along with surrounding tissues to reduce tumor burden and improve patient outcomes. Portal vein embolization is a pre-operative procedure aimed at enhancing liver function by redirecting blood flow, making patients more suitable for subsequent surgeries. Both methods aim to manage metastasis effectively and extend survival rates in affected individuals.</p></p>
