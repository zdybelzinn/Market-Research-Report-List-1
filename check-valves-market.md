<p><p>The check valves market is primarily segmented into three types: swing check valves, lift check valves, and others. Swing check valves utilize a swinging disc that allows flow in one direction while preventing backflow. Lift check valves feature a movable disc that lifts off its seat to permit flow and returns to close against back pressure. Other types include diaphragm and ball check valves, catering to specific applications and fluid characteristics. These variations enhance efficiency and reliability in fluid systems.</p></p>
