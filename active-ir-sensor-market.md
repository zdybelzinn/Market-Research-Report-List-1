<p><p>The Active Infrared (IR) Sensor market primarily consists of two types: Thermal and Quantum sensors. Thermal IR sensors detect heat emitted by objects, making them suitable for applications like security systems and industrial monitoring. They rely on the temperature differences in a scene to produce images or signals. In contrast, Quantum IR sensors leverage the interaction of infrared radiation with semiconductor materials to achieve higher sensitivity and resolution, ideal for scientific research and high-precision applications. Both types serve various industries, enhancing detection capabilities.</p></p>
