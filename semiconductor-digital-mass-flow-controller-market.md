<p><p>The Semiconductor Digital Mass Flow Controller market can be categorized into two main types: Thermal Type and Pressure Type. Thermal Type controllers measure flow by detecting temperature changes in a heated element, providing high accuracy and responsiveness, making them ideal for low flow applications. Pressure Type controllers, on the other hand, utilize differential pressure sensors to determine mass flow rates, offering robustness for higher flow ranges. Both types are critical in semiconductor manufacturing for process control and ensuring precise material delivery.</p></p>
