<p><p>Pressure-Volume Loop Systems in the cardiovascular market analyze various hemodynamic parameters. Ejection Fraction indicates the percentage of blood pumped from the heart per beat. Max and Min Ventricular dP/dt assess the rate of pressure change, reflecting contractile function. Cardiac Output measures total blood flow, while the Contractility Index evaluates myocardial contractility. Elastance relates to stiffness during contraction, and Stroke Volume indicates blood volume ejected per heartbeat. Pre-load Recruitable Stroke Work assesses the efficiency of stroke work with changes in preload.</p></p>
