Wet storage cabinets utilize materials that can handle moisture and feature drainage systems, ideal for gases that might produce condensate. In contrast, dry storage cabinets are built with materials resistant to corrosion and designed to maintain a moisture-free environment, essential for sensitive gases. Both types ensure compliance with safety regulations, enhancing workplace safety and reducing the risk of accidents involving gas cylinders.</p></p>
