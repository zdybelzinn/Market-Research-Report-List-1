<p><p>Mass Flow Controllers (MFCs) play a crucial role in the semiconductor equipment market by precisely regulating gas flow rates during manufacturing processes. Thermal MFCs utilize temperature differentials to measure and control flow, offering high accuracy for low-flow applications. In contrast, Pressure MFCs operate based on pressure differentials, making them suitable for higher flow rates and varying gas compositions. Both types ensure optimal process conditions, enhancing performance and yield in semiconductor fabrication, thereby driving advancements in technology and production efficiency.</p></p>
