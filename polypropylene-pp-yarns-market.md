<p><p>The polypropylene (PP) yarns market is primarily divided into two types: multifilament and monofilament yarns. Polypropylene multifilament yarn comprises numerous filaments twisted together, offering flexibility, strength, and moisture resistance, making it ideal for applications like textiles and industrial products. In contrast, polypropylene monofilament yarn consists of a single, thicker filament, providing rigidity and durability, suitable for uses such as fishing lines, nets, and certain packaging materials. Both types leverage polypropylene's lightweight and chemical-resistant properties.</p></p>
