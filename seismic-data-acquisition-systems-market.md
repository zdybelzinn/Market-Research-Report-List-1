<p><p>Seismic data acquisition systems are essential for geophysical surveys and exploration. The market is divided into two main types: wireless connected systems and wire connected systems. Wireless connected systems utilize radio frequencies or satellite communication, allowing for enhanced mobility and flexibility in data collection, especially in remote locations. In contrast, wire connected systems rely on physical cables for data transmission, offering stable connections and higher data integrity but may limit mobility. Each system caters to different operational needs and environments in seismic exploration.</p></p>
