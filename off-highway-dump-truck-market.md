<p><ul><li>Rigid Frame</li><li>Articulating Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The off-highway dump truck market consists of two primary types: rigid frame and articulating frame trucks. Rigid frame dump trucks have a single, solid chassis that offers enhanced stability and greater payload capacity, making them suitable for rough terrains and large construction projects. In contrast, articulating frame dump trucks feature a pivot point between the cab and the dump box, allowing for improved maneuverability in tight spaces and uneven surfaces. Both types cater to different operational needs in industries such as mining and construction.</p></p>
