<p><ul><li>Linear AC DC converters</li><li>SMPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC-DC converter chip market is primarily divided into two types: Linear AC-DC converters and Switch Mode Power Supply (SMPS) converters. Linear converters provide a steady output voltage with minimal noise, suitable for low-power applications, but are less efficient and generate more heat. In contrast, SMPS converters utilize high-frequency switching techniques, allowing for higher efficiency and compact designs, making them ideal for a wide range of applications, especially in consumer electronics and industrial systems. Both serve crucial roles in modern power management.</p></p>
