<p><strong>The Wood Preservers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood preservers market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based products. Water-based preservatives are environmentally friendly, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and quick drying times, making them suitable for indoor applications. Solvent-based preservatives, on the other hand, penetrate the wood more deeply, providing enhanced durability and protection against pests and decay. They typically have higher VOC levels and longer drying times, making them ideal for outdoor usage where strong resistance is needed.</p></p>
