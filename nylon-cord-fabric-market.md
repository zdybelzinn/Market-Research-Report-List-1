<p><strong>The Nylon Cord Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nylon 6</li><li>Nylon 66</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nylon cord fabric market is primarily divided into two main types: Nylon 6 and Nylon 66. Nylon 6 is produced through a process called ring-opening polymerization, resulting in a material known for its flexibility and resilience, making it ideal for various applications, including textiles and automotive components. Nylon 66, synthesized from hexamethylenediamine and adipic acid, offers enhanced strength and thermal resistance, making it suitable for industrial textiles, tire cords, and high-performance applications, where durability is critical.</p></p>
