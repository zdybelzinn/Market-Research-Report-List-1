<p><ul><li>Optical Scanning</li><li>Vein Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handprint scanner market is primarily divided into two types: optical scanning and vein scanning. Optical scanning uses high-resolution cameras to capture the unique patterns and characteristics of a person's handprint, making it suitable for applications like identity verification. In contrast, vein scanning identifies the unique patterns of blood vessels beneath the skin's surface, providing enhanced security and accuracy. Both technologies play crucial roles in biometric authentication across various sectors, including security, finance, and access control.</p></p>
