<p><p>The building film materials market includes various types of plastic films used in construction applications. LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and strength, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) provides moisture resistance. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is known for durability. PP/BOPP (Polypropylene/Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) features good clarity and strength. PET/BOPET (Polyethylene Terephthalate/Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) offers excellent barrier properties. Polyamide (BOPA) is known for toughness, while PVB (Polyvinyl Butyral) is used in laminating films. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile, used in various applications. Other materials encompass emerging and specialized films.</p></p>
