<p><p>Swing reflux valves are specialized devices used in various industrial applications to control the flow of fluids and prevent backflow. These valves operate based on a swinging gate mechanism, allowing fluid to flow in one direction while automatically closing to prevent reverse flow. Their design ensures minimal pressure drop and high flow efficiency, making them essential in sectors such as water treatment, oil and gas, and chemical processing.</p><p>The Swing Reflux Valves Market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 12.5% during the forecast period.
