<p><p>Home water filtration systems are categorized into several types, each designed to tackle specific contaminants. Reverse Osmosis (RO) filtration uses a semi-permeable membrane to remove dissolved solids and impurities, ensuring high-quality drinking water. UV filtration employs ultraviolet light to disinfect water, effectively eliminating bacteria and viruses. Carbon filtration utilizes activated carbon to absorb chemicals, chlorine, and odors, enhancing taste. Other market types may include gravity filters, water distillers, and ceramic filters, offering varied solutions to meet diverse needs and preferences.</p></p>
