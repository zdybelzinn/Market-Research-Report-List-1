<p><p>The Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) market is divided into two primary types: Anode PECVD Systems and Cathode PECVD Systems. Anode PECVD Systems utilize an anode to generate plasma, facilitating film deposition on substrates at lower temperatures, ideal for sensitive materials. In contrast, Cathode PECVD Systems employ a cathode, providing higher energy densities that enhance film quality and uniformity for various applications. These systems cater to diverse industries, including electronics, optics, and solar cell manufacturing.</p></p>
