<p><strong>The Laser Distance Measuring Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Triangulation Sensor</li><li>Time-of-Flight Laser Ranging Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser distance measuring sensors are primarily categorized into two types: Laser Triangulation Sensors and Time-of-Flight Laser Ranging Sensors. Laser Triangulation Sensors determine distances by measuring the angle of reflection from a laser beam directed at a target, making them suitable for short-range measurements with high precision. In contrast, Time-of-Flight Sensors calculate distance by measuring the time it takes for a laser pulse to travel to an object and back, enabling accurate distance measurements over longer ranges.</p></p>
