<p><p>The antimicrobial paint market features three main types based on active antibacterial agents. Natural antibacterial agents are derived from plant or mineral sources and leverage their inherent properties to inhibit microbial growth. Organic antibacterial agents utilize carbon-based compounds to offer antimicrobial effects, often featuring bioactive ingredients. In contrast, inorganic antibacterial agents comprise metal-based or non-carbon compounds, such as silver or copper, known for their long-lasting effectiveness against a broad spectrum of bacteria and pathogens. Each type caters to specific applications and user preferences.</p></p>
