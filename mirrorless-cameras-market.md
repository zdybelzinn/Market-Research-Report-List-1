<p><strong>The Mirrorless Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Sensor</li><li>CMOS Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mirrorless camera market predominantly features two sensor types: CCD and CMOS. CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors are known for their superior image quality and low noise, making them ideal for low-light conditions. Conversely, CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) sensors offer faster readout speeds, which enhances performance in burst shooting and video capture. CMOS sensors are also more energy-efficient, contributing to longer battery life. As a result, CMOS has gained popularity in mirrorless cameras, driving innovation and versatility in the market.</p></p>
