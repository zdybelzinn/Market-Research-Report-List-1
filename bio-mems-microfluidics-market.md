<p><p>Bio-MEMS (Biomedical Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) and microfluidics are innovative technologies that integrate mechanical, electronic, and fluidic components at the microscale, enabling precise manipulation of biological samples. Bio-MEMS devices are tailored for diverse applications such as drug delivery, diagnostics, and biosensing, while microfluidics focuses on the control and movement of fluids in channels ranging from tens to hundreds of micrometers.
