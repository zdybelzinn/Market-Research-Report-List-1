<p><ul><li>Standard Fistula Needles</li><li>AV Fistula Needles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The safety fistula needles market is divided into two main types: standard fistula needles and arteriovenous (AV) fistula needles. Standard fistula needles are used for conventional dialysis access, allowing blood flow from the patient to the dialysis machine. AV fistula needles are specifically designed for patients with arteriovenous fistulas, enhancing blood flow and minimizing complications. Both types prioritize patient safety, with features like safety mechanisms to prevent needle stick injuries, ensuring a safer dialysis experience for patients and healthcare providers.</p></p>
