<p><strong>The Electrohydraulic Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nozzle Flapper Valve</li><li>Jet Action Valve</li><li>Direct Drive Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrohydraulic valves market includes three primary types: nozzle flapper valves, jet action valves, and direct drive valves. Nozzle flapper valves utilize a flapper that opens or closes around a nozzle to control fluid flow, ideal for precise applications. Jet action valves operate by directing jets of hydraulic fluid, providing rapid switching and robust performance. Direct drive valves feature a direct mechanism for valve actuation, offering enhanced responsiveness and reduced latency, making them suitable for high-speed operations in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
