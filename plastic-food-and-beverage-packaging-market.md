<p><p>The plastic food and beverage packaging market is primarily divided into flexible and rigid packaging types. Flexible packaging includes materials like bags, pouches, and wraps that can easily conform to the shape of the product, offering lightweight and space-saving benefits. Rigid packaging, on the other hand, consists of inflexible containers such as bottles, jars, and boxes, providing stronger structural support and protection. Both types are crucial for preserving product freshness, enhancing shelf life, and ensuring consumer convenience.</p></p>
