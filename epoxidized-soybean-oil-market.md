<p><ul><li>Mode of Production: Ring Opening Polymerization</li><li>Mode of Production: Reaction with Maleic Anhydride</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxidized soybean oil market can be categorized by production modes: ring-opening polymerization and reaction with maleic anhydride. Ring-opening polymerization involves the conversion of epoxidized oil into polyfunctional polymers, enhancing its performance in various applications. In contrast, the reaction with maleic anhydride modifies the oil's properties, creating a versatile plasticizer for use in coatings, adhesives, and sealants. Both methods aim to improve the functional characteristics and sustainability of soybean oil-based products in diverse industries.</p></p>
