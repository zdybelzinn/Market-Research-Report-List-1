<p><p>Liquid mass flow controllers are essential instruments in the semiconductor market, designed to regulate the flow of liquids with precision. Thermal mass flow controllers utilize temperature differences to measure flow rates, offering high accuracy for small flow applications. In contrast, Coriolis mass flow controllers measure the mass flow directly by detecting changes in vibration frequency as liquid flows through a vibrating tube, providing excellent accuracy and reliability across a broader range of flow rates. Both types enable precise control in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
