<p><p>Urea-Formaldehyde (UF) adhesive resins for wood are classified into two main types based on their properties and applications. Type I resins offer higher resistance to moisture and are suitable for interior applications where humidity levels are controlled. This classification helps manufacturers select the appropriate adhesive for specific end-use scenarios, ensuring optimal performance and longevity of wood products.</p></p>
