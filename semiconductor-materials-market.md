<p><p>The semiconductor materials market comprises various categories, primarily including fab materials and packaging materials. Fab materials encompass the substrates, chemicals, and gases used during the fabrication of semiconductor devices in cleanroom environments. These materials are essential for lithography, etching, and doping processes. On the other hand, packaging materials are used to encase the finished semiconductor chips, providing protection and facilitating electrical connections. Both categories are critical for the performance, reliability, and lifespan of semiconductor products in various applications.</p></p>
