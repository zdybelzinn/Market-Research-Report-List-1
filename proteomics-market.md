<p><p>Proteomics is the large-scale study of proteins, particularly their functions and structures. This field involves the analysis of protein expression, modification, interaction, and localization, all of which are essential for understanding biological processes and disease mechanisms. Technological advancements in proteomic analysis tools and software have significantly enhanced the efficiency and accuracy of protein studies.
